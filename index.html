About Me

Computers have been a part of my life for as long as I can remember. My father was a CPA and a programmer back in the day of IBM Mainframes, and he used to let my big brother work at the office in the summers and do data entry. I began use a personal computers around 1995 and watched of the growth of operating systems, RAM and CPU speeds, and hard drive capacity as each new operating system demanded more resources. In the several years I've become interested in Web Design and have been learning and using CSS and HTML and am becoming familiar with Javascript.With some help from Bootstrap, I have made a few websites and am dedicated to becoming an expert in front end web develpment. My dream is to do this full time, and I am putting in the work to make this possible. Below, you'll see examples of what I've done so far.
